Energy Efficiency

Through the green project, energy management can become more efficient as we work together with partners who can provide visibility of usage and quality management to ensure waste energy is minimized.

As for water management, the green project has vast experience in the industry and we aim to tackle the opportunity to establish NRW technology and utilize its information to tackle early detection of leakages. Based on data by Modor Intelligence, Indonesia has high opportunity for the growth of NRW, with 351 drinking water BUMD as a target for this technology. Simultaneously we aim to assist the government in reducing NRW to achieve its 25% target by 2024.

Energy Management System (EMIS)

- Help manage and analyze energy usage to create forecast and assist implementation
- Dashboard, monitoring, control, analysis
- Tools: monitoring data analysis, energy information system, fault detection and diagnostic tools, automated system optimization
- Use data accurately to manage energy performance and efficiency
