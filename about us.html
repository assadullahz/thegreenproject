The Green Project

Building a Better Future

The Green Project is part of Catur Elang Perkasa’s mission to create a better future across Southeast Asia through green project. We believe that the return of green investment exceeds profitability, where sustainability will contribute to a better living environment for years to come.

Our Vision

We aspire to become the leader in implementing green projects to maintain the natural balance of various potential areas in Southeast Asia.

Our Mission
  1. Establish a portfolio of sustainable projects across Southeast Asia as a fundamental step to mitigate the impact of climate change
  2. Synergize the implementation of each project by utilizing advanced green technology
  3. Carry out green projects by optimizing capex and opex
