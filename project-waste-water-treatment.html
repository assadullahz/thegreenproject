Wastewater Treatment

Water Treatment: Pasuruan faces limited water sources, relying on well drilling or surface water via SPAM. Regulations control deep well water usage, allocating 5-10% for communities through PDAM rules. Areas like Samarinda, Gresik, and Sidoarjo struggle due to water supply-demand gaps, quality issues, distribution challenges, and environmental impacts during development. Projects focus on energy-efficient technology and adapting to water sources (e.g., peat river in Dumai, springs in Pasuruan) for distribution spanning 9 km and 40 km.
